Play Games

All work and no play makes jack a dull boy.
Whack-A-Mole
A game of Whack-A-Mole. You struck the mole (but in this case, an apple) as it shows up on each cell as the time counts down. Each struck increase your score by one.
Memory (flip image) Game
A game where you click on each cell to reveal the image and click on another to reveal a corresponding image. Getting a similar image gives a count of 1, but getting a wrong image means play again.
